Caricatures

Make everyone at your party feel like a STAR, for 5 minutes each. I bring everything I need to create an authentic caricature booth, including my own chair and lighting. I'm a longtime Warner Bros. / DC Comics artist, able to draw everything from classic portraits to fun cartoons, themes, group shots, work from photos, even pets. Let me graphic design the paper with your party info to personalize each piece of art.
  • Rates start at $165 / hr.
  • No deposit required.
  • Serving the entire LA metro area, IE and valleys.
  • 12-15 faces / hr.
  • Sturdy card stock in a clear plastic sleeve.
  • Frames and hard vinyl sleeves available.



Commissions

Order a full-color portrait for you and your family. Just email me photos and your theme idea and I will create a memory that lasts a lifetime, and deliver it framed anywhere in LA.


Theme Parties

Perfect for astronaut, dinosaur, sports, Disney princess, Monster Truck, Marvel superhero, and other theme parties. I bring template paper with characters matching your theme, leaving enough room to draw your guests' heads on top. Everyone will be amazed by what I create in minutes. Add a custom sticker I graphic design and get a personalized gift to remember the event.




Coloring Parties

An all-ages art activity party using my B/W coloring pages. I bring all the materials and draw along with your guests.




Your guests color their own artwork. I visit each artist, show appreciation, demonstrate techniques, add flair and make sure everyone goes home with a drawing they love, proud that they made it themselves.





  • Rates start at $175 / hr.
  • 1-hour ($175) deposit required.
  • Serving the entire LA metro area, IE and valleys.
  • I bring tubs of markers, art stix, lap boards, paper and bags
  • I create coloring pages to match your party theme
  • Frames and hard vinyl sleeves available.
